This app is for the kids. Help us make a nation of happy kids! -- week 6

17 February 2015 on update

This app is for the kids.

Since the beginning, me2 has been all about the kids. We have had the privaledge to meet some super pre-teen/teenager kids along with parents, counselors, life coaches, youth organizations who all simply care about kids. This is great and all, but what matters truly in the end is that the kids are happy.

Team member Isaac acting during our video shoot.

First off, it simply has blown me away to see how many people are out there who are trying to make a difference for kids. People dedicating their lives to kids every single day not expecting anything in return. I have repeatedly called up family and friends after talking to a group of people and told them about how amazed I was with what that particular group is doing for kids. It is such an honor to personally meet these wonderful people!

Because of the great feedback that we have been receiving, we are starting to see trends in the feedback. We are discovering what kids search for when they seek support and we have some ideas how we can make me2 satisfy those needs. This week we started to take a step back and began to dig a little deeper into how me2 is going to benefit the kids. We are not done asking questions yet! More research needs to be done but progress is definitely being made!

Lets make a nation of happy kids. A me2 nation!

Like I mentioned last week, me2 is entering in the Dream Big, Grow Here contest. We have entered the contest and uploaded our video to check out. The reason we are entering the Dream Big, Grow Here contest is because it is a great way to share our dream to everyone. We want to create a nation full of happy kids. We want to build a me2 nation!

If we win the grant prize, we plan to use the money to cover technical, legal, and marketing costs in order to launch me2 on Android, iOS and the web. The remainder of the money will be used for growth of the product to reach as many kids as possible on the platform. We hope that kids fall in love with me2 and find happiness when using it. We don't want to deny this great tool to a child if we can help it.

Everyone is allowed to vote for the contest once a day every single day up until March 1st when the voting period ends.

Every young person struggles at some point in life. We are here to say #me2. You are not alone. @me2nation
