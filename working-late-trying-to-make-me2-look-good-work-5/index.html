Working late trying to make me2 look good. -- work 5

09 February 2015 on update

This week in the world of me2

Short post this week. The me2 team was out of the office most of the week meeting with counselors, youth organizations, and some kids. Besides that, the creative and product team was up working late trying to make me2 look good. Working on a new website, a video, and a new logo. Yes, a new logo!

Check out our new digs!

Getting a new logo for your business is always exciting. We have been working on a new logo for a few weeks now trying to get the style and colors just right.

Out with the blue and in with the green. With me2, we want to be known for an uplifting product. Having an uplifting product means having a fun and uplifting logo. We were looking to create a logo with smooth soft edges and fun calming colors to match. We think this new logo is a step in the right direction. Hope you like it as much as we do!

Our new website is coming along.

We are tired of looking at that ugly splash page website just as much as you are (especially because it has the old logo on it now, yuck!). I was working most of my week this week trying to fix that. I cannot share much with you yet, but I will say it is coming very shortly!

me2 is entering the Dream Big, Grow Here contest!

From their website: "Dream Big, Grow Here is an award winning business grant contest for Iowans to fulfill their dreams of self-employment or expansion of their current firm." We are entering for that exact reason, we have a dream to create a nation of happy kids. We began creating a video for the contest a couple weeks ago and since then, we have been fixing it up here and there. *February 16th it will be available online for you to view and vote for us in the contest! *

Every young person struggles at some point in life. We are here to say #me2. You are not alone. @me2nation
